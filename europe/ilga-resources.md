# 🌈 ILGA Resources & Rainbow Map

ILGA (International Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Trans and Intersex Association) provides some of the most comprehensive data on LGBTQ+ rights worldwide. Here's how to use their resources to research your next destination.

## ILGA Europe — Rainbow Map

The **Rainbow Map** ranks 49 European countries on LGBTI equality across 6 categories:

1. **Equality and non-discrimination**
2. **Family**
3. **Hate crime and hate speech**
4. **Legal gender recognition**
5. **Intersex bodily integrity**
6. **Asylum**

**🔗 Access the live map**: [ILGA Europe Rainbow Map](https://www.ilga-europe.org/resources/rainbow-map)

## ILGA World — State-Sponsored Homophobia Report

The **State-Sponsored Homophobia** report maps sexual orientation laws globally:
- **🔗 [ILGA World Maps](https://ilga.org/maps-sexual-orientation-laws)**
- Tracks criminalization, protections, and recognition laws
- Updated annually

### Key Trans-Specific Resources from ILGA

1. **Trans Legal Mapping Report**
   - Comprehensive global survey of trans-specific laws
   - Covers legal gender recognition, name changes, healthcare access
   - 🔗 [ILGA Trans Report](https://ilga.org/trans-report)

2. **Trans Murder Monitoring**
   - Global data on reported murders of trans people
   - 🔗 [TGEU Trans Murder Monitoring](https://transrespect.org/en/trans-murder-monitoring/)

3. **Global Regulations Database**
   - Searchable database of LGBTQ+ laws by country
   - 🔗 [ILGA Database](https://database.ilga.org/)

## How to Use ILGA Data for Relocation Research

### Step 1: Check the Rainbow Map
- Go to the [Rainbow Map](https://www.ilga-europe.org/resources/rainbow-map)
- Click on any country to see detailed scores
- Focus on **Legal Gender Recognition** and **Hate Crime** categories

### Step 2: Read the Country Report
- Each country has a full legal report
- Look for:
  - ✓ Self-ID laws
  - ✓ Informed consent healthcare
  - ✓ Anti-discrimination protections
  - ✓ Hate crime laws

### Step 3: Cross-Reference with Our Guides
- Use our **[Country Guides](/europe/)** for practical information on:
  - Healthcare access and clinics
  - Immigration pathways
  - Community resources
  - Cost of living

### Step 4: Verify Current Information
- Laws change frequently — always verify with official sources
- Contact local LGBTQ+ organizations for current conditions
- Join expat LGBTQ+ forums for firsthand experiences

## Limitations of ILGA Data

While ILGA provides excellent legal analysis, it doesn't cover:
- **Healthcare quality** and wait times
- **Social acceptance** in different regions
- **Cost of living** and economic factors
- **Practical immigration processes**
- **Community resources** and support networks

Use our **Country Guides** and **Global Index** alongside ILGA data for comprehensive research.
