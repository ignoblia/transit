# Belgium

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.2/5**

### Why Belgium?
- **Legal**: Self-declaration gender recognition since 2018, strong anti-discrimination protections
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent model with good public healthcare coverage for trans care
- **Safety**: Very safe country with strong hate crime laws protecting gender identity
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ communities in Brussels, Antwerp, and Ghent
