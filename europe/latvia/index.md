# Latvia

## Quick Verdict
Latvia has some of the most restrictive gender recognition laws in the EU, requiring surgery for legal change. Healthcare access is very limited, and social conservatism remains strong outside Riga.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐ (2/5)**

### Why Latvia?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — requires diagnosis + surgery for gender marker change
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping with very limited trans-specific services
- **Safety**: Moderate — Riga more tolerant, rural areas conservative
- **Community**: Small but active LGBTQ+ scene in Riga; Baltic Pride rotates through Latvia
