# Healthcare in the Netherlands

## Gender Affirming Healthcare

The Netherlands has a well-established system for gender affirming healthcare, primarily through specialised **gender teams at university medical centres (UMCs)**. The country follows a stepwise protocol that has evolved toward an informed consent model, though some structured assessment remains.

## HRT Access

Hormone therapy is accessed through gender teams at UMCs. While the Netherlands historically used a gatekeeping model, the approach has become increasingly patient-led:
- Initial intake with a psychologist/psychiatrist (typically 1-3 sessions)
- No longer requires full RLE (real life experience)
- Endocrine assessment and hormone initiation
- Follow-up monitoring every 3-6 months initially
- Typical wait times: 4-12 months (varies by clinic)

## Surgical Options

The Netherlands has one of the most advanced gender surgery programmes in Europe, particularly at Amsterdam UMC:
- **Chest/breast surgery (top surgery)** — Widely available, covered by insurance
- **Genital surgery (bottom surgery)** — Amsterdam UMC is a world-leading centre
- **Facial feminisation surgery** — Partial insurance coverage
- **Voice surgery** — Available but limited coverage
- **Hysterectomy/oophorectomy** — Covered

Insurance (basisverzekering) covers gender affirming treatments under the basic healthcare package for those with a diagnosis of gender dysphoria.

## Prescription Portability

As an EU member state, the Netherlands accepts prescriptions from other EU/EEA countries:
- Valid for up to 30 days
- English-language prescriptions recommended
- Dutch pharmacies may require a Dutch GP to re-prescribe for ongoing treatment

| Medication | Typical Cost (insured) | Pharmacy Availability |
|------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Oestradiol (oral/gel/patches) | Covered/€5-15 eigen risico | Widely available |
| Testosterone (gel/injectable) | Covered/€10-25 eigen risico | Widely available |
| Cyproterone acetate | Covered/€8-15 eigen risico | Available |
| GnRH agonists | Covered (specialist) | Specialist pharmacy |

## Mental Health Support

- **Gender team psychologists** — Integrated into UMC care
- **Transgender Network Nederland** — Peer support
- **Private gender therapists** — Faster access, costs €80-150/session
