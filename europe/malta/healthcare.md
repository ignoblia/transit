# Healthcare in Malta

## Gender Affirming Healthcare

Malta provides gender affirming healthcare through its public healthcare system, with the primary clinic at **Mater Dei Hospital** in Msida. The **Gender Wellbeing Clinic** is the national referral centre for transgender healthcare.

## HRT Access

Malta follows an informed consent model for hormone therapy:
- No requirement for a formal diagnosis of gender dysphoria
- Self-referral to the Gender Wellbeing Clinic
- Treatment is free through the public healthcare system
- Private endocrinologists also offer informed consent care
- Typical wait times: 1-3 months for initial appointment

### Key Clinics

| Clinic | Location | Services | Languages | Wait Times |
|--------|----------|----------|-----------|------------|
| Gender Wellbeing Clinic - Mater Dei | Msida | HRT initiation, monitoring, psychological support | EN, MT | 1-3 months |
| Mater Dei Hospital - Endocrinology | Msida | Hormone therapy, monitoring | EN, MT | 1-2 months |
| Private Endocrinologists | Various | Informed consent HRT, faster access | EN | 1-4 weeks |

## Surgical Options

Gender affirming surgeries are available through the public system with some procedures covered. Options include:
- Chest/breast surgery (top surgery) — Covered with criteria
- Genital surgery (bottom surgery) — Referral to EU specialists possible
- Facial feminisation surgery — Limited public coverage
- Hysterectomy/oophorectomy — Covered

For complex surgeries, Malta utilises the **EU Cross-Border Healthcare Directive** to refer patients to specialised centres in other EU countries with costs covered.

## Prescription Portability

As an EU member state, Malta accepts prescriptions from other EU/EEA countries under EU regulations. Key points:
- EU prescriptions valid for up to 30 days
- Recommended to carry a doctor's letter in English
- Emergency supply without prescription possible in urgent cases

| Medication | Typical Cost (public) | Pharmacy Availability |
|------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Oestradiol (oral/gel) | Free/€10-15 private | Widely available |
| Testosterone (injectable/gel) | Free/€15-30 private | Widely available |
| Spironolactone | Free/€8-12 private | Available |
| GnRH agonists (puberty blockers) | Free (with approval) | Specialist order |

## Mental Health Support

- **Gender Wellbeing Clinic** — Integrated psychological support
- **MGRM** — Peer support and counselling referrals
- **Private psychologists** — Several specialise in gender identity
