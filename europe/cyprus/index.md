# Cyprus

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐2.5/5**

### Why Cyprus?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — diagnosis required, surgery sometimes still required
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping with very limited trans-specific services
- **Safety**: Moderate — socially conservative, but legal protections exist
- **Community**: Small LGBTQ+ community centred in Nicosia, Limassol, Larnaca
