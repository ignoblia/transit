## Key Factors Explained

### Legal Gender Recognition
- **Self-ID**: Ability to change legal gender through self-declaration without medical intervention.
- **Medicalized**: Requires psychiatric diagnosis, hormone therapy, or surgery for legal recognition.

### Healthcare Access
- **Informed Consent**: Access to hormone replacement therapy (HRT) based on informed consent model.
- **Gatekeeping**: Requires psychiatric approval, long waiting periods, or other barriers.

### Safety & Legal Protections
- Includes hate crime laws, anti-discrimination protections, and general safety for trans individuals.

### Digital Nomad Feasibility
- Availability of remote work visas, digital infrastructure, and ease of staying long-term for remote workers.
