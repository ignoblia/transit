# Netherlands

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.3/5**

### Why Netherlands?
- **Legal**: Self-ID model adopted in 2025, replacing the previous medicalised system
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent at major university medical centres
- **Safety**: Strong anti-discrimination protections and social acceptance
- **Community**: Active and visible LGBTQ+ community in Amsterdam, Utrecht, Rotterdam
