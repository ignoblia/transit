# Healthcare in Cyprus

## Gender Affirming Healthcare

Cyprus has very limited trans-specific healthcare services. The healthcare system operates a **gatekeeping model** and there are no dedicated gender identity clinics on the island.

## HRT Access

### Requirements

- Psychiatric diagnosis of gender dysphoria
- Referral to an endocrinologist
- Limited specialists with trans healthcare experience
- Most services are in Nicosia and Limassol

### Process

1. **Psychiatric Assessment** — Find a psychiatrist with trans experience (very few available)
2. **Diagnosis** — Gender dysphoria diagnosis
3. **Referral** — To an endocrinologist for HRT
4. **HRT Initiation** — After medical evaluation and blood work
5. **Monitoring** — Regular follow-up with endocrinologist

### Key Providers

| Provider | Location | Services | Notes |
|----------|----------|----------|-------|
| General Hospital Nicosia | Nicosia | Limited trans services | Psychiatry, endocrinology |
| General Hospital Limassol | Limassol | Limited trans services | Psychiatry, endocrinology |
| Private psychiatrists | Nicosia/Limassol | Assessment, diagnosis | Few with trans experience |
| Private endocrinologists | Nationwide | HRT prescription | Limited trans knowledge |
| Accept Cyprus referrals | Nationwide | LGBTQ+ referral network | Very helpful for finding providers |

### Challenges

- **No dedicated gender clinics** on the island
- Very few psychiatrists and endocrinologists with trans healthcare experience
- Healthcare professionals often lack training in trans health
- Many trans people travel abroad for healthcare (Greece, UK, Germany)
- Stigma concerns in smaller communities
- Appointment availability is limited

## Prescription Portability

- **EU member** — EU prescription cross-border rules apply
- EU/EEA prescriptions valid for up to 30 days
- Cypriot prescriptions filled at local pharmacies
- Non-EU prescriptions require doctor verification
- HRT medications (oestradiol, testosterone) available at pharmacies

## Insurance Coverage

Cyprus has the **General Healthcare System (GESY)** :

- Universal healthcare coverage for residents
- Trans-related care included in principle
- HRT medications partially covered
- GP and specialist visits covered (small co-payment)
- Surgery coverage requires approval
- Private insurance available for supplemental coverage

## Surgical Options

- **Very limited surgical options** in Cyprus
- Most trans people travel abroad for surgery:
  - **Greece**: Common destination for surgeries
  - **Thailand**: Popular for bottom surgery
  - **Spain/UK/Germany**: For other procedures
- Insurance may contribute to overseas surgery costs with prior approval
- Top surgery: limited availability, mostly private
- Bottom surgery: not available locally

## Mental Health Support

- **Accept Cyprus** — LGBTQ+ counselling and referral service
- **Cyprus Family Planning Association** — Counselling services
- Private therapists: very few with trans experience
- Online therapy options available
- Peer support groups through Accept Cyprus

## Costs

| Service | Typical Cost | GESY Coverage |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| GP visit | Covered | Yes (small co-pay) |
| Psychiatric assessment | €50-100 (private) | Partial |
| Endocrinology consult | Covered (public) / €60-120 (private) | Partial |
| HRT (monthly) | €15-40 | Partial |
| Blood tests | Covered | Yes |
| Private therapy | €50-80 | Not covered |
