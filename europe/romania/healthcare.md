# Healthcare & HRT in Romania

## Gatekeeping Model
Romania's transgender healthcare is **gatekept at a single institute** in Bucharest:

- **Psychiatric and endocrinological diagnosis required** for access to HRT
- Primary center: Institutul de Endocrinologie "C.I. Parhon" in Bucharest
- Services concentrated almost entirely in **Bucharest** and **Cluj-Napoca**
- Very limited trans-competent providers

## Accessing HRT

### Step 1: Initial Consultation
- Contact **Institutul de Endocrinologie "C.I. Parhon"** in Bucharest
- Psychiatry department for gender dysphoria evaluation
- Some private psychiatrists in Bucharest and Cluj can provide assessments
- Multiple sessions required over several months

### Step 2: Endocrinology Assessment
- Referral to endocrinology department at the same institute
- Baseline blood tests and medical history review
- Discussion of treatment goals
- Diagnosis of gender dysphoria

### Step 3: HRT Commencement
- Hormones prescribed after diagnosis and assessment
- Follow-up visits every 3-6 months
- Ongoing monitoring of hormone levels
- Some GPs can manage ongoing care after specialist establishes treatment

## Key Clinics

| Clinic | Location | Services |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Institutul de Endocrinologie "C.I. Parhon" | Bucharest | Comprehensive trans care |
| Regina Maria Private Healthcare | Bucharest, Cluj | Endocrinology (limited trans experience) |
| MedLife Private Healthcare | Bucharest | Endocrinology, psychiatry |
| Emergency County Hospital | Cluj-Napoca | Limited endocrinology |

## Prescription Portability
- **EU prescriptions**: Valid in Romania under EU mutual recognition
- **Non-EU prescriptions**: Must be re-evaluated by a Romanian doctor
- **Importing medication**: 3-month supply permitted with a doctor's letter
- **Medication availability**: Generally good in major cities
- **Pharmacies**: Major chains (Catena, Sensiblu, Help Net) nationwide

## Surgical Options
- **No gender-affirming surgeries available** in Romania
- All surgeries require travel abroad
- No public funding for gender-affirming surgery
- Some Romanian surgeons perform top surgery on a private basis (very limited, results vary)
- Bottom surgery: Not available domestically

## Costs

| Service | Romanian Resident | Non-Resident |
|---------|------------------|--------------|
| Psychiatric assessment (public) | Free | Limited access |
| Endocrinology visit (public) | Free | Limited access |
| Private psychiatric assessment | €40-80 | €40-80 |
| Private endocrinology | €40-70 | €40-70 |
| HRT (monthly) | €10-25 | €15-35 |
