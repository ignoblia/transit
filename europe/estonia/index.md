# Estonia

## Quick Verdict
Estonia is a safe, digitally advanced Baltic nation with good trans visibility, though legal gender recognition remains medicalized despite a proposed reform. The e-Residency and Digital Nomad Visa make it accessible for remote workers.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)**

### Why Estonia?
- **Legal**: Medicalized model (diagnosis + HRT required); new self-ID law proposed but not passed
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping at clinics in Tallinn and Tartu
- **Safety**: Generally good, progressive urban centers, Russian-speaking minority
- **Community**: Growing LGBTQ+ scene in Tallinn; Baltic Pride rotates through Estonia
