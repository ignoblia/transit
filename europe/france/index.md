# France

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐3.8/5**

### Why France?
- **Legal**: Self-declaration gender recognition since 2022, no surgery or diagnosis required
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent model available, though varies by practitioner
- **Safety**: Good overall, but varies by region; Paris is very LGBTQ+ friendly
- **Community**: Large, vibrant LGBTQ+ community, especially in Paris, Lyon, Marseille
