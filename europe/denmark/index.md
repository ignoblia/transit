# Denmark

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.3/5**

### Why Denmark?
- **Legal**: First European country to allow self-ID (2014), strong legal protections
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent model, though wait times can be significant
- **Safety**: Extremely safe, very low violent crime, trans-friendly urban areas
- **Community**: Vibrant LGBTQ+ scene in Copenhagen, growing in Aarhus
