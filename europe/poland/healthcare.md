# Healthcare & HRT in Poland

## Gatekeeping Model
Poland's transgender healthcare operates through a **sexuology-based gatekeeping model**:

- **Psychiatric/sexuology diagnosis required** for access to HRT
- Services concentrated in major cities (Warsaw, Kraków, Poznań, Wrocław)
- Long wait times for initial appointments (4-12 months)
- Growing but still very limited trans-competent providers

## Accessing HRT

### Step 1: Sexuology Assessment
- Contact a sexuology clinic (public or private) in a major city
- Public options: Institute of Psychiatry and Neurology (Warsaw), university hospitals
- Private options: Available with shorter wait times but higher costs
- Diagnosis of gender dysphoria per ICD-10/11
- Multiple sessions over 3-6 months

### Step 2: Endocrinology Referral
- Referral to an endocrinologist for hormone therapy
- Baseline blood tests required
- HRT prescribed and monitored
- Follow-up visits every 3-6 months initially

### Step 3: Ongoing Care
- Regular monitoring by endocrinologist
- Some GPs may manage HRT after specialist establishes treatment
- Medications generally available at pharmacies

## Prescription Portability
- **EU prescriptions**: Valid in Poland under EU mutual recognition
- **Non-EU prescriptions**: Must be re-evaluated by a Polish doctor
- **Importing medication**: 3-month supply permitted with a doctor's letter
- **Medication availability**: Generally good in major cities
- **Pharmacies**: Major chains (Ziko, Apteka, DOZ) nationwide

## Surgical Options
- **Very limited surgical options** in Poland
- Some top surgery available (primarily private)
- Bottom surgery: Very limited, virtually unavailable domestically
- Most travel abroad (Thailand, Germany, Belgium, Spain)
- No public funding for gender-affirming surgery
- Private top surgery: €3,000-6,000
- Private bottom surgery abroad: €15,000-25,000

## Costs

| Service | Polish Resident | Non-Resident |
|---------|----------------|--------------|
| Sexuology assessment (public) | Free | Limited access |
| Sexuology assessment (private) | €50-100 | €50-100 |
| Endocrinology visit (public) | Free | Limited access |
| Endocrinology visit (private) | €40-80 | €40-80 |
| HRT (monthly) | €10-30 | €15-40 |
