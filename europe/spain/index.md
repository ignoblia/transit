# Spain

## Quick Verdict
Spain offers excellent legal protections for transgender individuals, including self-determination gender recognition and comprehensive healthcare access.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐½ (4.5/5)**

### Why Spain?
- **Legal**: Self-ID since 2023 (Law 3/2023)
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent in many regions
- **Safety**: Very good, strong anti-discrimination laws
- **Community**: Large LGBTQ+ community, massive Pride events
