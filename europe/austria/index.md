# Austria

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.0/5**

### Why Austria?
- **Legal**: Self-ID model adopted in 2024, replacing medicalized system
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent in Vienna (AKH), gatekeeping in some regions
- **Safety**: Very safe country with strong social protections
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scene in Vienna, Graz, Linz, Salzburg
