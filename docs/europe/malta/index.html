Malta ​

Quick Verdict ​

Overall Rating: ⭐4.6/5

Why Malta? ​

  • Legal: Constitutional protections for gender identity since 2015
  • Healthcare: Informed consent model at Mater Dei Hospital
  • Safety: Consistently rated safest country in Europe for LGBTIQ+ people
  • Community: Vibrant LGBTQ+ scene, English widely spoken
