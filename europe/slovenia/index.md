# Slovenia

## Quick Verdict
Slovenia is a progressive, safe Central European country with good social acceptance. Legal gender recognition remains medicalized but is accessible. Healthcare is centralized at the University Medical Centre in Ljubljana.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)**

### Why Slovenia?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — requires diagnosis + HRT, surgery not explicitly required
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping at University Medical Centre Ljubljana
- **Safety**: Good — liberal, progressive society, strong rule of law
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scene in Ljubljana; Ljubljana Pride
