# Portugal

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.2/5**

### Why Portugal?
- **Legal**: Self-determination model for gender recognition since 2018
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent model with some remaining gatekeeping
- **Safety**: One of the safest countries in the world
- **Immigration**: Multiple visa options including D7 passive income and D8 digital nomad visas
