# United Kingdom

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐3.0/5**

### Why the UK?
- **Legal**: Medicalized Gender Recognition Act 2004, reform stalled
- **Healthcare**: NHS gatekeeping with 2-5 year waiting times; private options available
- **Safety**: Moderate — varies significantly by region (Scotland better)
- **Community**: Large LGBTQ+ communities in London, Brighton, Manchester, Glasgow
