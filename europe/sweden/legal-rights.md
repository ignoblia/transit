# Legal Rights in Sweden

## Gender Recognition

Sweden adopted a self-determination model for legal gender recognition through a new law that took effect in 2025. Previously, Sweden required a gender dysphoria diagnosis and a waiting period. The sterilisation requirement was removed in 2013.

The current (2025) process:
- Application to the **Swedish Tax Agency (Skatteverket)**
- No medical certificates or diagnosis required
- No surgery requirement
- No waiting period
- For minors aged 12-17, application with parental consent and psychosocial support
- Minors under 12 require special consideration with parental consent

This reform was the result of many years of advocacy and represents a significant shift from Sweden's historically medicalised approach.

## Name Changes

Name changes can be submitted alongside the gender recognition application to Skatteverket. Sweden has a very permissive name change policy — any first name can be registered regardless of gender marker. For name-only changes (without gender marker change), an online application to Skatteverket is sufficient.

## Anti-Discrimination Laws

Sweden has comprehensive anti-discrimination protections:
- **Discrimination Act (Diskrimineringslagen)** — Explicitly prohibits discrimination based on gender identity and gender expression
- **Employment** — Full protection in all employment aspects
- **Education** — Protection in schools and universities
- **Healthcare** — Right to access care without discrimination
- **Housing** — Protection in rental and purchase
- **Goods and services** — Comprehensive protection

The **Equality Ombudsman (Diskrimineringsombudsmannen - DO)** enforces discrimination laws.

## Hate Crime Laws

Sweden's Penal Code (Brottsbalken) includes hate crime provisions with enhanced penalties for crimes motivated by a person's gender identity or gender expression. This covers:
- Violent crimes
- Threats and harassment
- Hate speech (hets mot folkgrupp)
- Property crimes

Swedish police have specialised hate crime units, and reporting mechanisms are well-established.
