# Healthcare & HRT in Bulgaria

## Gatekeeping Model
Bulgaria has **extremely limited** transgender healthcare and **no dedicated gender clinics**:

- **Psychiatric diagnosis required** to access any trans healthcare
- No specialized transgender health services exist
- Healthcare providers have very little experience with trans patients
- Almost all services are concentrated in **Sofia**

## Accessing HRT

### Step 1: Psychiatric Assessment
- Contact a psychiatrist (public or private) in Sofia
- Very few psychiatrists specialize in or have experience with gender dysphoria
- Multiple sessions typically required over several months
- Diagnosis per ICD-10/11

### Step 2: Endocrinology
- No dedicated trans endocrinology services
- General endocrinologists may prescribe HRT with a psychiatric referral
- Most endocrinologists have little to no experience with trans patients
- You may need to educate your provider about treatment protocols

### Step 3: Ongoing Care
- HRT monitoring by endocrinologist
- Follow-up visits every 3-6 months
- Blood tests at general laboratories
- Medication availability can be inconsistent

## Key Clinics

| Clinic | Location | Services |
|--------|----------|----------|
| University Hospital Alexandrovska | Sofia | Psychiatry, endocrinology (limited trans experience) |
| Military Medical Academy | Sofia | Psychiatry referrals |
| Private psychiatrists | Sofia, Plovdiv | Assessment (shorter wait times) |
| Private endocrinologists | Sofia | HRT (very limited trans experience) |

## Prescription Portability
- **EU prescriptions**: Valid in Bulgaria under EU mutual recognition
- **Non-EU prescriptions**: Must be re-evaluated by a Bulgarian doctor
- **Importing medication**: 3-month supply permitted with a doctor's letter
- **Stock issues**: Some HRT medications may require special ordering
- **Pharmacies**: Major chains (Sopharmacy, VChele) in larger cities

## Surgical Options
- **No gender-affirming surgeries available** in Bulgaria
- All surgeries require travel abroad
- No public funding for gender-affirming surgery
- No established referral pathways for surgery abroad

## Costs

| Service | Bulgarian Resident | Non-Resident |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|
| Psychiatric assessment | Free (public) | €50-100 |
| Endocrinology visit | Free (public) | €50-80 |
| HRT (monthly) | €15-40 | €20-50 |
| Private psychiatrist | €30-60 | €40-80 |
