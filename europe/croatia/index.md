# Croatia

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐2.7/5**

### Why Croatia?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — requires diagnosis, 1 year HRT, and court approval
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping with limited clinics and long waiting times
- **Safety**: Moderate — conservative rural areas, but tourist regions more tolerant
- **Community**: Small but active LGBTQ+ scene in Zagreb, Rijeka, Split
