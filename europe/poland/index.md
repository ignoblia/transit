# Poland

## Quick Verdict
Poland's transgender situation varies significantly by region. Legal gender recognition relies on inconsistent court decisions, often appealed by the government. Large cities like Warsaw and Kraków offer better safety and some healthcare, but "LGBTQ+ free zones" exist in many municipalities. Not recommended overall.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐ (2/5)**

### Why Poland?
- **Legal**: Medicalized with no clear framework — court decisions vary by region and are frequently appealed by the government
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping at sexuology clinics with long wait times
- **Safety**: Low to moderate — "LGBTQ+ free zones" in some municipalities, Warsaw/Kraków more tolerant
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scene in major cities
