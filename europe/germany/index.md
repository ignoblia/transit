# Germany

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.1/5**

### Why Germany?
- **Legal**: Self-Determination Act (SBGG) effective 2025, landmark self-ID law
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent varies by region, excellent public health coverage
- **Safety**: Generally very safe, strong anti-discrimination protections
- **Community**: Large LGBTQ+ communities in Berlin, Cologne, Hamburg, Munich
