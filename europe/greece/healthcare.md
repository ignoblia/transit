# Healthcare & HRT in Greece

## Gatekeeping Model
Greece operates a **gatekeeping model** for transgender healthcare:

- **Psychiatric diagnosis required** to access HRT
- Assessment at specialized Gender Identity Clinics
- Treatment monitored by a multidisciplinary team
- Process varies between public and private sectors

## Accessing HRT

### Step 1: Referral
- Obtain a referral from a GP or directly contact a Gender Identity Clinic
- The main public clinic is at **Alexandras General Hospital** in Athens
- Thessaloniki has limited services at AHEPA Hospital

### Step 2: Psychiatric Assessment
- Diagnosis of gender dysphoria per ICD-10/11
- Psychological evaluation over 3-6 sessions
- Family support assessment may be included

### Step 3: Hormone Therapy
- Referral to an endocrinologist after diagnosis
- Baseline blood tests and physical examination
- HRT prescribed under medical supervision
- Follow-up every 3-6 months

## Key Clinics

| Clinic | Location | Services |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Alexandras General Hospital | Athens | Multidisciplinary gender clinic (public) |
| AHEPA University Hospital | Thessaloniki | Limited gender services (public) |
| Private endocrinologists | Athens, Thessaloniki | HRT (shorter wait times) |
| IASIS Clinic | Athens | Private gender-affirming care |
| Metropolitan Hospital | Athens | Private surgical options |

## Prescription Portability
- **EU prescriptions**: Valid in Greece under EU mutual recognition
- **Non-EU prescriptions**: Must be evaluated and re-prescribed by a Greek doctor
- **Carrying medication**: 90-day supply permitted with a doctor's letter
- **Keep medication in original packaging** with pharmacy labels
- **Know generic/brand names**: Estrofem (estradiol), Androcur (cyproterone), Testogel (testosterone)

## Surgical Options
- Gender-affirming surgeries are **covered by public healthcare** (EFKA) with prior authorization
- Surgery requires referral from an authorized gender clinic
- Public wait times: 12-24 months for consultation, 18-36 months for surgery
- Private surgery: €5,000-15,000 depending on procedure
- Popular destinations: Athens, Thessaloniki, or abroad (Thailand, Spain, Serbia)

## Costs

| Service | Public (insured) | Private |
