# Bulgaria

## Quick Verdict
Bulgaria has the highest reported transphobia in the EU, with no clear legal framework for gender recognition, gatekept healthcare, and a deeply socially conservative society. Not recommended for transgender relocation under any circumstances.

**Overall Rating: ⭐ (1/5)**

### Why Bulgaria?
- **Legal**: Medicalized with no clear framework — relies on inconsistent court decisions
- **Healthcare**: Extremely limited, no dedicated gender clinics
- **Safety**: Lowest trans safety rating in the EU, widespread discrimination
- **Community**: Very small and underground; no major trans organizations
