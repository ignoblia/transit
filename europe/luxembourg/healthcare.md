# Healthcare in Luxembourg

## Gender Affirming Healthcare

Luxembourg provides gender affirming healthcare through its public healthcare system (Caisse Nationale de Santé - CNS). The primary centre for trans-specific healthcare is the **Centre Hospitalier de Luxembourg (CHL)**.

Healthcare is available in three official languages: Luxembourgish, French, and German. English is also spoken at major hospitals.

## HRT Access

Luxembourg follows an informed consent model for hormone therapy. This means:
- No requirement for a psychological assessment or diagnosis of gender dysphoria
- Patients can access hormone therapy after a consultation with an endocrinologist
- Treatment is covered by the public health insurance system (CNS)
- Typical wait times for an endocrinology appointment are 2-4 months

| Clinic | Location | Services | Languages | Wait Times |

## Surgical Options

Gender affirming surgeries are covered by the CNS under certain conditions. Prior authorisation is required and typically involves a multidisciplinary assessment. Available surgeries include:
- Chest/breast surgery (top surgery)
- Genital surgery (bottom surgery)
- Facial feminisation surgery (selective coverage)
- Voice surgery (limited coverage)

Patients may also access surgical care in neighbouring countries (France, Belgium, Germany) under the EU cross-border healthcare directive.

## Prescription Portability

As an EU member state, Luxembourg accepts prescriptions from other EU/EEA countries under the Cross-Border Healthcare Directive (2011/24/EU). Visitors with EU prescriptions can fill them at Luxembourgish pharmacies. It is recommended to carry a physical copy of the prescription and a letter from the prescribing doctor.

| Medication | Typical Cost (CNS covered) | Pharmacy Availability |
|------------|---------------------------|----------------------|
| Oestradiol (oral/gel) | ~€5-15/month | Widely available |
| Testosterone (injectable/gel) | ~€10-25/month | Widely available |
| Spironolactone | ~€8-12/month | Available |
| Antiandrogens (Cyproterone) | ~€10-20/month | Available |

## Mental Health Support

Psychological support for gender identity issues is available through:
- CHL Psychiatry Department
- Private psychologists specialising in gender identity
- CIGALE support groups
