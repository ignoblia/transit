# Norway

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.3/5**

### Why Norway?
- **Legal**: Self-determination for gender recognition since 2016
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent model at Oslo University Hospital
- **Safety**: Extremely safe with strong social acceptance
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scene in Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim
