# Hungary

## Quick Verdict
Hungary has completely banned legal gender recognition since 2023 and is actively hostile toward transgender people through government policy. The 2021 "child protection" law has fostered widespread discrimination. Not recommended for transgender relocation.

**Overall Rating: ⭐ (1/5)**

### Why Hungary?
- **Legal**: Complete ban on legal gender recognition since 2023 (2020 ban for minors)
- **Healthcare**: HRT available but very limited and under political pressure
- **Safety**: Low — government actively promotes anti-LGBTQ+ policies
- **Community**: Small, under pressure; Budapest more tolerant than rest of country
