# Romania

## Quick Verdict
Romania has a medicalized legal gender recognition system that requires diagnosis, HRT, and court approval. Healthcare access is gatekept at a single institute in Bucharest. Safety is moderate to low with conservative social attitudes, though Bucharest and Cluj-Napoca are more tolerant. The digital nomad visa is a notable option.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐ (2/5)**

### Why Romania?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — requires diagnosis + HRT + court approval, but recognized
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping at Institutul de Endocrinologie in Bucharest
- **Safety**: Moderate to low — conservative, but Bucharest/Cluj more tolerant
- **Community**: Small but active LGBTQ+ scene in Bucharest and Cluj
