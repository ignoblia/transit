# Switzerland

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.2/5**

### Why Switzerland?
- **Legal**: Medicalized system — reform pending with proposed self-ID bill
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent available at major university clinics
- **Safety**: One of the safest countries in the world
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scenes in Zurich, Geneva, Basel, Bern
