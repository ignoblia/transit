# Ireland

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐3.8/5**

### Why Ireland?
- **Legal**: Self-declaration gender recognition since 2015, one of the first EU countries
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping model, improving but long wait times for public care
- **Safety**: Generally very safe, progressive social attitudes
- **Community**: Strong LGBTQ+ community in Dublin, Cork, Galway
