# Czech Republic

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐2.8/5**

### Why the Czech Republic?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — requires diagnosis, sterilization or hormones, and name change. Reform attempted but stalled
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping at Sexuologický ústav in Prague, public insurance covers treatment
- **Safety**: Moderate — growing far-right influence but Prague is tolerant
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scene in Prague and Brno
