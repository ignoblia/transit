# Healthcare & HRT in Spain

## Informed Consent Model
Spain has a decentralized healthcare system, and trans healthcare access varies by **autonomous community**:

- **Informed consent model** available in most progressive regions
- **No psychiatric diagnosis required** in regions with informed consent protocols
- Some regions may still require a mental health professional's letter

## Accessing HRT

### Step 1: Find Your Local Unit
- Contact the public hospital's **Gender Identity Unit** (Unidad de Identidad de Género) in your region
- Private clinics also available with shorter wait times

### Step 2: Initial Assessment
- Medical history and treatment goals discussed
- Blood tests for baseline hormone levels
- Fertility preservation options discussed (available through public system)

### Step 3: Treatment
- HRT prescribed after assessment
- Follow-up every 3-6 months initially
- Public system covers hormone medications

## Key Clinics

### Public Hospitals
- **Hospital Clínic** — Barcelona (Catalonia)
- **Hospital Universitario La Paz** — Madrid
- **Hospital Virgen del Rocío** — Seville (Andalusia)
- **Hospital Vall d'Hebron** — Barcelona
- **Hospital General de Valencia** — Valencia

### Private Options
- **IM Gender** — Madrid & Barcelona
- **Clínica Dres. Sánchez** — Madrid
- **Various private endocrinologists** in major cities

## Prescription Portability
- **EU prescriptions**: Valid across Spain under EU mutual recognition
- **Non-EU prescriptions**: Must be evaluated by Spanish doctor
- **Carrying medication**: Keep original packaging with prescription labels
- **Generic names**: Know the generic names of your medications for local refills

## Surgical Options
- Gender-affirming surgeries covered by **public healthcare** (Seguridad Social)
- Referral from Gender Identity Unit required
- Wait times: 12-24 months for initial consultation
- Private options available: 6,000-15,000€ depending on procedure
- Popular destinations for surgery: Barcelona, Madrid, Malaga

## Costs
- **Public healthcare**: HRT and consultations are free (tax-funded)
- **Prescriptions**: Minimal co-pay (10% of cost, capped)
- **Private care**: 100-200€ per consultation
