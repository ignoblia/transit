# Healthcare in the United Kingdom

## Gender Affirming Healthcare

The UK operates a **gatekeeping model** through the National Health Service (NHS). Gender Identity Clinics (GICs) require a diagnosis of gender dysphoria before treatment. **Waiting times are among the longest in Europe** — 2-5 years for a first appointment at most NHS GICs.

## NHS Process

1. GP referral to a Gender Identity Clinic
2. **First appointment**: 2-5 year wait. Initial assessment with a psychiatrist/psychologist
3. **Diagnosis**: Gender dysphoria diagnosis after several appointments (typically 3-6 months)
4. **Hormone referral**: Referred to endocrinology
5. **HRT**: Endocrinology appointment — typically 3-6 months after diagnosis
6. **Follow-up**: Regular monitoring, typically every 6-12 months

## Private Options

Due to long NHS waits, many trans people in the UK use private healthcare:

| Provider | Services | Cost | Wait Time |
|----------|----------|------|-----------|
| **GenderGP** | Online-only, informed consent model | £30/month subscription + £150 setup | 2-4 weeks |
| **Gendercare** | London-based, psychiatrist + endocrinologist | £300-500 per appointment | 3-6 months |
| **Gender Doctors** | Online + London | £300-500 per appointment | 1-3 months |
| **YourGP** | Edinburgh/online | £250-400 per appointment | 1-3 months |
| **London Transgender Clinic** | London | £200-350 per appointment | 2-4 months |
| **The Gender Clinic** | Manchester/online | £300-400 per appointment | 2-4 months |

### Bridging Prescriptions

- Some GPs will provide bridging prescriptions while waiting for NHS GIC
- This is at the GP's discretion
- Available through **GenderGP Shared Care** if GP agrees

## Prescription Portability

- **Not in EU** — EU prescription cross-border rules do not apply
- Private prescriptions from GenderGP, Gendercare etc. can be dispensed at UK pharmacies
- NHS prescriptions cost £9.90 per item (England)
- Scotland, Wales, NI: Free prescriptions
- HRT medications are generally available at all pharmacies

| Medication | NHS Cost | Private Cost |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| Oestradiol (oral/gel/patches) | £9.90/item | £10-30/month |
| Testosterone (gel/injectable) | £9.90/item | £20-50/month |
| GnRH agonists (puberty blockers) | NHS covered | £80-120/month |
| Cyproterone acetate | £9.90/item | £15-30/month |

## Surgical Options

- **NHS surgery**: Requires referral from GIC, 2-4 year wait after referral
- **Private surgery**: Available through private hospitals
- **Chest surgery (top surgery)**: NHS covered, wait 1-3 years; Private £6,000-10,000
- **Genital surgery (bottom surgery)**: NHS covered, wait 2-4 years; Private £20,000-40,000
- **FFS**: Very limited NHS coverage, private £10,000-40,000
- **Voice therapy**: NHS available, wait 1-2 years; private £300-800

## Mental Health Support

- **GIC psychology teams**: Included in assessment process
- **Mind**: LGBTQ+ mental health support
- **Switchboard LGBT**: Helpline 0300 330 0630
- **Mermaids**: Youth support (under 25)
- **Gendered Intelligence**: Counselling and peer support
- London Transgender Clinic: Private counselling £80-150/session
