# Lithuania

## Quick Verdict
Lithuania has restrictive gender recognition requiring diagnosis, HRT, and surgery. A Constitutional Court ruling has made legal changes even harder. Healthcare access is limited to Santaros Clinics in Vilnius.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐ (2/5)**

### Why Lithuania?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — requires diagnosis + HRT + surgery; Constitutional Court ruling increased barriers
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping at Santaros Clinics (Vilnius) only
- **Safety**: Moderate — growing conservative influence, Vilnius more tolerant
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scene in Vilnius; Baltic Pride rotates through Lithuania
