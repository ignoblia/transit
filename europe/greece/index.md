# Greece

## Quick Verdict
Greece offers a moderate environment for transgender individuals. Legal gender recognition is medicalized but does not require surgery. Healthcare access is limited to major cities, and social attitudes are more conservative outside Athens and Thessaloniki.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)**

### Why Greece?
- **Legal**: Medicalized under Law 4491/2017 — diagnosis required, no surgery needed
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping at Alexandras Hospital (Athens); limited clinics elsewhere
- **Safety**: Moderate — Athens and Thessaloniki more tolerant, rural areas conservative
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ organizations, especially in Athens
