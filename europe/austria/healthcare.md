# Healthcare in Austria

## Gender Affirming Healthcare

Austria has a mixed model for trans healthcare. The **AKH Vienna (Allgemeines Krankenhaus)** operates an informed consent approach, while other regions, particularly smaller cities and rural areas, may still require psychiatric gatekeeping.

## HRT Access

### Vienna — AKH (Informed Consent)

The **Medical University of Vienna / AKH** operates an informed consent model:

- **Gender outpatient clinic (Genderambulanz)** at the Department of Gynaecology
- Clinical psychologists and endocrinologists work together
- Focus on patient autonomy
- No mandatory psychiatric diagnosis
- English-speaking staff available

### Process at AKH Vienna

1. Self-referral or GP referral to the Genderambulanz
2. Initial consultation: discussion of treatment goals, medical history
3. Psychological consultation (1-2 sessions — not diagnostic, but supportive)
4. Endocrinology consultation and blood tests
5. Informed consent form signed
6. HRT prescription — typically within 2-4 months of first contact
7. Regular follow-up every 3-6 months

### Regional Variations

Outside Vienna, the model varies:

- **Graz**: LKH Graz — Evaluation-based, psychiatric diagnosis often required
- **Linz**: Kepler University Hospital — Mixed approach
- **Salzburg**: Salzburg University Hospital — Gatekeeping more common
- **Innsbruck**: Medical University of Innsbruck — Limited trans services
- **Smaller cities**: Often require psychiatric referral, limited specialists

## Prescription Portability

- **EU member state** — EU prescription cross-border rules apply
- EU/EEA prescriptions valid for up to 30 days
- Austrian doctors may re-prescribe for ongoing treatment
- Non-EU prescriptions require verification
- German language prescriptions preferred; English accepted
- **ÖGK (Österreichische Gesundheitskasse)** covers trans-related care

## Insurance Coverage

Austria has mandatory **social health insurance (Krankenversicherung)**:

- **ÖGK** covers gender-affirming treatments
- HRT medications covered with prescription (prescription fee ~€6-10 per item)
- Surgery coverage requires medical necessity approval
- Co-payment (Rezeptgebühr) is €6.50 per prescription (2024)
- Annual cap on co-payments

## Surgical Options

- Gender-affirming surgeries available at AKH Vienna and some regional hospitals
- Insurance covers surgeries after medical necessity established
- Wait times: 6-12 months for consultation, 12-18 months for surgery
- Top surgery: covered, wait 6-12 months
- Bottom surgery: covered, wait 12-18 months
- FFS: limited coverage

## Mental Health Support

- **AKH Genderambulanz** — Integrated psychological support
- **HOSI Wien** — LGBTQ+ counselling and peer support
- **Trans* Austria** — Community support groups
- Private gender therapists: €80-150/session
