# Slovakia

## Quick Verdict
Slovakia requires sterilization for legal gender recognition, which is considered a human rights violation. Healthcare is limited to Bratislava, and social conservatism is strong. While Bratislava offers some tolerance, the country's legal requirements make it not recommended for transgender relocation.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐ (2/5)**

### Why Slovakia?
- **Legal**: Medicalized — requires diagnosis + sterilization, very restrictive
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping, limited trans care only in Bratislava
- **Safety**: Moderate — Bratislava more tolerant, rural areas conservative
- **Community**: Small LGBTQ+ scene in Bratislava; Košice has limited community
