# Luxembourg

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐4.2/5**

### Why Luxembourg?
- **Legal**: Self-determination model for gender recognition since 2018
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent model at CHL with hormone therapy access
- **Safety**: Very safe country with strong anti-discrimination protections
- **Community**: Small but growing LGBTQ+ community, particularly in Luxembourg City
