# Sweden

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐3.8/5**

### Why Sweden?
- **Legal**: Self-ID model adopted in 2025, replacing the previous medicalised system
- **Healthcare**: Public healthcare with long wait times at ANOVA clinic
- **Safety**: Very safe country with strong social welfare
- **Community**: Active LGBTQ+ scene in Stockholm, Gothenburg, Malmö
