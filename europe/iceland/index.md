# Iceland

## Quick Verdict
Iceland ranks as one of the safest and most trans-friendly countries in the world, with progressive gender recognition laws and excellent healthcare access.

**Overall Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)**

### Why Iceland?
- **Legal**: Self-ID since 2019 (Gender Autonomy Act)
- **Healthcare**: Informed consent model at Landspítali Hospital
- **Safety**: Consistently ranked safest country globally
- **Community**: Strong LGBTQ+ organizations and social acceptance
