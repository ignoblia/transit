# Healthcare in Portugal

## Gender Affirming Healthcare

Portugal provides gender affirming healthcare through its **public healthcare system (Serviço Nacional de Saúde - SNS)**. The system operates under an informed consent model but retains some gatekeeping elements, particularly for surgeries. Access to HRT is relatively straightforward.

## HRT Access

Hormone therapy in Portugal:
- Consult with a GP or directly at a hospital endocrinology department
- Informed consent approach — no formal diagnosis required for HRT
- Some hospitals may still request a psychological assessment
- Treatment is free or low-cost through the SNS
- Private endocrinologists offer faster access
- Typical wait times: 2-6 months (public), 1-4 weeks (private)

## Surgical Options

Gender affirming surgeries are available through the SNS, though waiting lists can be long:
- **Chest/breast surgery (top surgery)** — Covered by SNS, wait times 1-2 years
- **Genital surgery (bottom surgery)** — Covered, limited local capacity, often referred abroad
- **Facial feminisation surgery** — Limited public coverage
- **Hysterectomy/oophorectomy** — Covered
- **Voice surgery** — Very limited availability

Portugal uses the **EU Cross-Border Healthcare Directive** to refer patients for surgeries unavailable domestically.

## Prescription Portability

As an EU member state, Portugal accepts prescriptions from other EU/EEA countries:
- Valid for up to 30 days
- English or Portuguese prescriptions recommended
- Ongoing treatment should be transferred to a Portuguese GP/endocrinologist

| Medication | Typical Cost (SNS covered) | Pharmacy Availability |
|------------|--------------------------|----------------------|
| Oestradiol (oral/gel/patches) | Free/€5-10 private | Widely available |
| Testosterone (gel/injectable) | Free/€10-20 private | Widely available |
| Spironolactone | Free/€5-10 private | Available |
| Cyproterone acetate | Free/€8-15 private | Available |

## Mental Health Support

- **SNS psychology services** — Available through hospital gender clinics
- **ILGA Portugal** — Counselling and support groups
- **Private therapists** — Gender-competent therapists available, €40-70/session
- **rede ex aequo** — Youth support services
