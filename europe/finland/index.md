# Finland

## Quick Verdict

**Overall Rating: ⭐3.9/5**

### Why Finland?
- **Legal**: Self-ID law passed in 2023, modernizing gender recognition
- **Healthcare**: Gatekeeping model is improving, but wait times remain long
- **Safety**: One of the safest countries in the world
- **Community**: Active but smaller LGBTQ+ community, strong in Helsinki
